Frequently Asked Questions

What type of projects do you take on? +
We specialize in high-end residential, commercial offices, and hospitality spaces. We focus on projects where design integrity is paramount.
How does the design process work? +
It begins with a discovery call, followed by concept development, detailed design presentation, and finally implementation and styling.
What is the minimum project budget? +
To ensure the highest quality of materials and execution, our minimum project budget typically starts at ₦5M.

Yes, we offer virtual design consultations and full-service remote project management for clients outside our immediate location.
