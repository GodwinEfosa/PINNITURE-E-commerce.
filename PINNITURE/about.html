Who We Are

Our Story

What began as a small woodworking studio has evolved into a premier furniture and interior design house. We believe that every space tells a story, and our role is to provide the perfect chapters — pieces that speak to heritage, intention, and beauty.

From hand-selecting ethically sourced timber to delivering white-glove installation, every step of our process is guided by a relentless commitment to quality. Our team of master craftsmen and designers collaborate to produce furniture that doesn't just fill a room — it defines it.

Today, Pinniture serves homes, offices, and commercial spaces across Nigeria and beyond, carrying the same spirit of craftsmanship that inspired our very first piece.

What Drives Us

Our Value

The principles that shape every decision we make and every piece we build

Solid Craftsmanship

Every joint, finish, and detail is held to uncompromising standards. We hand-finish each piece with the precision of an artisan and the soul of an artist.

Sustainability

We use 100% FSC-certified timber and low-VOC eco finishes, ensuring that our beautiful work does not come at the expense of the planet.

Community

We are proud to support local artisans and maintain fair-trade partnerships, investing in the communities that give our work its authentic character.

Design Integrity

Form and function are never in conflict here. Every design decision serves both the beauty of the space and the practical life lived within it.
