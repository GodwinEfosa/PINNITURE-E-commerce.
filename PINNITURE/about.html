Who We Are

Our Story

We founded Pinniture with a simple, non-negotiable belief: that African homes and offices deserve furniture that is as thoughtfully designed and precisely built as anything produced anywhere in the world." Pinniture was born from a frustration with compromise. The furniture available in our markets was either too expensive for what it offered, too fragile for how we actually live, or too generic to reflect the sophistication of contemporary African citure and taste. We built a different model. By establishing production hubs in major Nigerian cities, we cut the distance between craftsman and client, between idea and execution. Our designers work closely with our makers. Our makers work closely with our clients. Nothing is outsourced to indifference. The result is a furniture and interiors brand that delivers genuine quality at a price that respects you and spaces that tell the story of who you are.

What We Stand For

Precision Over Speed

We will never rush a piece out of production at the expense of quality. Every Pinniture product is measured, tested, and finished to a standard we would put in our own homes. Lead times are honest, and delivery is exact.

Local First

Our materials are sourced locally wherever quality allows. Our craftsmen are Nigerian. Our hubs are embedded in the communities we serve. We believe in building an economy of excellence within our own borders.

Design That Endures

Trends fade. We design for permanence — furniture and spaces that feel timeless in five years the way they feel current today. Our aesthetic sits at the intersection of contemporary minimalism and the warmth of African material culture.

Honest Partnership

Every client relationship is a collaboration. We listen more than we speak. We sketch before we build. We revise until it's right. The goal is not to impose our taste — it is to realize your vision with our expertise.

What Drives Us

Our Values

The principles that shape every decision we make and every piece we build

Solid Craftsmanship

Every joint, finish, and detail is held to uncompromising standards. We hand-finish each piece with the precision of an artisan and the soul of an artist.

Sustainability

We use 100% FSC-certified timber and low-VOC eco finishes, ensuring that our beautiful work does not come at the expense of the planet.

Community

We are proud to support local artisans and maintain fair-trade partnerships, investing in the communities that give our work its authentic character.

Design Integrity

Form and function are never in conflict here. Every design decision serves both the beauty of the space and the practical life lived within it.
