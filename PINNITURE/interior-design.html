Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What is the typical timeline for a residential project?
Standard residential projects range from 8-16 weeks depending on scope. Custom furniture or structural changes may extend timelines. We provide a detailed schedule during the planning phase.
Do you work with existing furniture or only new pieces?
Absolutely! We love integrating cherished pieces into new designs. Our approach is collaborative—we assess what you have and build around it, supplementing with new items where needed.
What is your pricing structure?
We offer transparent, project-based pricing. After our free consultation, you'll receive a detailed proposal outlining design fees, procurement costs, and installation. No hidden charges.
Do you handle permits and contractor coordination?
Yes, for full-service projects. Our team manages all permits, contractor vetting, scheduling, and quality oversight. You enjoy a single point of contact throughout the process.
Can you work within my budget?
Definitely. We tailor solutions to your budget range, offering phased approaches and value-engineering options. Our goal is beautiful, functional design at every investment level.
